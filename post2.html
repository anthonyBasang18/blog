UC Taekwondo Jaguars captured the 2016 Panagbenga Luzon Taekwondo Championship.

Posted by silverbullet18 on March 5, 2016

University of Cordilleras Taekwondo team captured the 2016 Panagbenga Luzon Taekwondo Championship (Senior Division).University of Baguio placed 2nd which shared the same record, but by virtue of win-over-the-other rule, UC went on to claim its 1st Senior Division title. Holy Angels University place 3rd.

Ira John paris(blue) of University of Cordilleras dominates his opponent Justin Lopez(red) of Holy Angels University during championship game Both players are from the University of Cordilleras, Bond Gooban(blue) throws his skip 45 towards his opponent while Whinny Bayawon(red) release his turning long counter attact
